Lesson Pricing

At DVDS our driving lesson prices are very competitive. We offer the option to pay for your driving lessons on a weekly basis, or if you would rather pay for a block booking of 10 hours or more this can be done at a discounted rate.

When choosing a driving school we offer high-quality driving lessons at affordable prices. If you would like to purchase a gift voucher this can be for any amount. To pay for block payments/gift vouchers we accept cash/BACS directly to the instructor or if you would prefer you can pay via BACS to the office.

Please call for the office bank details. We can book your Theory Test and Driving Test for you at no extra cost. Please note that gift vouchers and block payments are valid for one year from the purchase date.
